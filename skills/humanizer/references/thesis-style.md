# Generic Academic Thesis Style Profile

Use this reference when the user asks to match an undergraduate academic thesis style,
prior academic writing, or a restrained scholarly voice.

This is an anonymized style profile. It intentionally excludes names, institutions,
identifiers, local paths, thesis titles, advisor names, unpublished data, project-specific
datasets, and other source-specific details. Use it as a writing-style guide only, not as
a source of claims.

## Chinese Voice

- Formal undergraduate thesis style, clear and evidence-oriented.
- Common structure: research background -> current limitation -> rationale -> study
  design -> multi-layer analysis -> cautious implication.
- Uses "本研究" frequently and appropriately.
- Defines abbreviations on first use with Chinese term + English full name + abbreviation.
- Prefers measured verbs: "比较", "构建", "探索", "考察", "评估", "提示", "有待进一步验证".
- Gives concrete details: sample size, grouping criteria, data type, material or method,
  database, normalization, statistical test, p value, and interpretation when the user
  provides them.
- Avoids overly emotional language; persuasive force comes from specificity.

## English Voice

- Compact academic abstract style.
- Typical sentence flow:
  1. Research context.
  2. Limitation of current indicator, model, or method.
  3. Data source and analysis method.
  4. Main statistical or descriptive results.
  5. Bounded implication.
- Uses "remains", "yet", "however", "was stratified", "were observed", "was associated
  with", "suggests", "providing evidence/reference".
- Keep this English restrained. Avoid making it sound like marketing copy.

## Preferred Chinese Patterns

- "[研究对象/问题]是……领域的重要研究对象。"
- "[指标/模型/方法]是目前常用的……，但其……仍有局限。"
- "近年来多项研究报道……，但针对……的系统比较仍然较少。"
- "本研究对……开展分析/比较/评估。"
- "结果表明……"
- "上述结果表明……，这一发现有助于……，也可为后续……提供参考。"

These are style anchors only. Do not force them where they do not fit.

## Preferred Academic Restraint

- Observational results should not be rewritten as causal mechanisms.
- Use "存在差异", "呈现趋势", "显著关联", "可能反映", "提示" rather than "证明".
- When p values are borderline, say "处于统计学显著性的边界" or "具有参考意义" only if
  supported by surrounding results.
- When discussing mechanisms, distinguish "已有研究提示" from "本研究证明".

## Domain-Neutral Sensitivities

- Preserve user-provided grouping labels, thresholds, strata, variables, and method names
  exactly.
- Preserve analysis levels exactly: descriptive metrics, differential features, networks,
  pathways, themes, categories, functional analysis, model evaluation, or any other levels
  named by the user.
- Keep the distinction between description, association, interpretation, and mechanism.
- Do not overgeneralize from a specific sample, corpus, case, or data group to a broader
  population.

## Quick Rewrite Checklist

- Does each paragraph have one main claim?
- Are technical terms defined once and then used consistently?
- Are all statistics, grouping labels, and citation markers unchanged?
- Are causal verbs avoided unless supported?
- Does the conclusion say what the result can support, not what the writer wishes it
  supported?
